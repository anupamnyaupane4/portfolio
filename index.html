About me

I am a passionate innovator and educator driven by the art of problem-solving—not just building solutions but discovering creative approaches to tackle challenges in diverse areas. Whether it's designing machines, crafting videos, or initiating projects, I pour my energy into creating impactful solutions that resonate with people.

My journey so far has been dynamic. I've built five machines, founded an organization, Navigo Nepal dedicated to bridging equity in exposure and opportunities for underprivileged students, and spearheaded groundbreaking initiatives like Nepal's first NFC card and a VR heritage package. As someone who's deeply expressive, I find joy in storytelling—be it through videos, music, or conversations. Beyond my work, I express myself through dance, sports like soccer and cricket, and music—playing the guitar is a personal favorite. This love for expression has fueled my ability to see the world through different perspectives, helping me craft solutions that address real needs.

Now, I aim to channel this creativity and vision toward shaping global education systems—creating tangible, transformative results that empower individuals and communities. Each step has deepened my commitment to driving change.Learning is my lifelong passion, and I believe that innovation combined with empathy has the power to redefine how we educate and inspire the next generation.
